*  Day 3 of playing the natural number game
** Log
I used two hints and I got
- "nth_rewrite 2 [two_eq_succ_one] is I think quicker than rw
[two_eq_succ_one]."
- "Now you can rw [add_succ]"

Here's my proof
#+begin_src lean
nth_rewrite 2 [two_eq_succ_one]
rw[add_succ]
rw[four_eq_succ_three]
nth_rewrite 2 [succ_eq_add_one]
rw[three_eq_succ_two]
rw[succ_eq_add_one]
rw[succ_eq_add_one]
rfl
#+end_src

Here's an example proof the website gave
#+begin_src lean
nth_rewrite 2 [two_eq_succ_one] -- only change the second `2` to `succ 1`.
rw [add_succ]
rw [one_eq_succ_zero]
rw [add_succ, add_zero] -- two rewrites at once
rw [← three_eq_succ_two] -- change `succ 2` to `3`
rw [← four_eq_succ_three]
rfl
#+end_src


The key thing I learned is that you can change both sides.
I was under the impression that I had to change only the right side to
match the left side exactly.
